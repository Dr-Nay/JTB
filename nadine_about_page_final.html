My Story

"Success taught me everything except how to truly live. As a CEO and pastor, I learned that achievement without alignment leaves you empty, no matter how impressive the résumé looks. The breakthrough came when I chose courage over comfort, embracing the sacred work of honest self-examination. Because here's what I discovered: the strongest leaders aren't those who avoid pain, but those who transform it into purpose."

My journey through loss, growth, spiritual awakening, and pastoral calling led me to write The Journey to Becoming: Keys to Personal Growth and Transformation, a biblically-grounded guidebook born from years of real-world experience leading organizations, pastoring souls, and discovering that true transformation requires both spiritual depth and practical wisdom.

This isn't just theory on pages, it's a roadmap forged through my own journey of becoming. From becoming the first college graduate in my father's lineage to celebrating achievements that became stepping stones to even greater purpose, from weathering personal storms and professional setbacks to discovering that our greatest breakdowns often become our greatest breakthroughs. Through leading in boardrooms and shepherding souls, from building businesses to building faith, from writing this book over four years of personal transformation to completing my doctorate while navigating family tragedy, every chapter has been lived, tested, and proven in the crucible of real life.

Today, I help others begin their own transformational journey through my writing, pastoral ministry, speaking, and meaningful conversations rooted in biblical truth. My mission is to equip you with practical wisdom, spiritual insights grounded in Scripture, and real-world strategies that create lasting change in your life, because transformation isn't just a destination, it's a way of becoming.

About The Journey to Becoming

The Journey to Becoming: Keys to Personal Growth and Transformation is more than a book; it's your companion for the most important journey you'll ever take. Through eleven transformative chapters, you'll discover the biblical foundations for lasting change, from mastering self-discipline and mindset transformation to navigating God's silence and unleashing the power of forgiveness.

  • The painful silence of God – and how to navigate it.
  • Forgiving others (and yourself) without losing your mind.
  • Rebuilding your identity after disappointment.
  • Conquering fear-based decisions with Spirit-led clarity.
  • Practicing daily spiritual disciplines that work in real life.
